How to Play

  • Freestyle to your heart's content on the Play page
  • Open the sheet music page in a separate tab to follow along
  • Challenge yourself by playing against the computer on the Challenge page
  • The notes are shown above and below the corresponding keys on the piano, represented by the musical note symbol

Using the Virtual Piano

  • Tap the keys on a touchscreen device
  • Click on the keys with your mouse
  • Use your computer keyboard.
  • The computer keyboard keys are shown above and below the corresponding keys on the piano, represented by the keyboard symbol
