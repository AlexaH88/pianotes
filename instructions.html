How to Play

  • Freestlye Mode: click on the Freestyle button on the Play page to play the piano freely as you like.
  • Sheet Music: open the sheet music page in a separate tab to follow along. You can also listen to an audio sample of the song if you're unfamiliar with the music.
  • Challenge Mode: click on the Challenge button on the Play page to play a 'Simon' style game against the computer. You can find the rules of the game
    here.Simon Game Rules

Using the Virtual Piano

  • Computer mouse: click on the piano keys with your mouse to play it.
  • Computer keyboard: use your computer keyboard to play the piano keys, as shown on each piano key next to the keyboard symbol .
  • Touchscreen: tap the keys on a touchscreen device.
